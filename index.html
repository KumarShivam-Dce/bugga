The BUGGA Story

Born from Experience. Built for You.

After spending years teaching at India's top coaching institutes like Motion and Sri Chaitanya, our founder, Bipin Bihari (IIT BHU), realized a harsh truth: the traditional coaching industry has become a factory. Thousands of students are packed into halls, losing their identity and their confidence.

BUGGA (Bihari's Ultimate Growth and Guidance Academy) was established to break this cycle. We bring the elite pedagogy of Kota and Hyderabad right to Muzaffarpur, ensuring every student gets the premium guidance they deserve.

Our Vision

To build an institution where every single student is intellectually challenged and emotionally supported. We aim to nurture the brightest minds into tomorrow's IITians and ethical medical professionals.

Why BUGGA is Different

We stripped away the marketing fluff and kept only what actually helps a student crack competitive exams.
